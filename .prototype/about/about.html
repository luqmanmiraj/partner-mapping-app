About

NEXUS is the leading global automotive aftermarket community. With more than 637 distributor members, 4,103 WDs and 9,953 retail stores affiliated in 146 countries, allied with 90+ global manufacturers suppliers, NEXUS is a growth accelerator for its community.

Established in 2014 by CEO Gaël Escribe, NEXUS Automotive International, the leading automotive aftermarket (AA) global community, is shaping the future of the AA.
